# Recruiting Benchmark Report Sources

This file lists the canonical set of recruiting and TA benchmark reports to
collect during Phase 1. For each source, fetch the primary URL and follow any
prominent PDF or "download report" links.

---

## Ashby

**Publisher**: Ashby (ATS & analytics platform)
**Focus**: Funnel metrics, offer acceptance, time-to-hire, by company size/stage
**Primary URL**: https://www.ashbyhq.com/resources/talent-trends-report
**Alternate / archive**: https://www.ashbyhq.com/resources
**Notes**: Usually published Q1. Very TA-centric; covers funnel conversion,
recruiter capacity, and pipeline velocity. One of the most cited reports in
tech recruiting.

---

## Gem

**Publisher**: Gem (sourcing & CRM platform)
**Focus**: Outreach response rates, source-of-hire mix, pipeline conversion
**Primary URL**: https://www.gem.com/resource/recruiting-benchmarks
**Alternate**: https://www.gem.com/resources (filter for "benchmarks" or "report")
**Notes**: Strong on outreach data (InMail response rates, email open rates,
sequence efficacy). Tends to be skewed toward tech/mid-market companies.

---

## LinkedIn

**Publisher**: LinkedIn Talent Solutions
**Focus**: Global hiring trends, talent supply/demand, skill gaps, recruiter sentiment
**Primary URL**: https://business.linkedin.com/talent-solutions/global-talent-trends
**Alternate**: https://www.linkedin.com/business/talent/blog/talent-strategy/global-talent-trends
**Notes**: Large global sample. Good for macro trends and labor market context.
Less granular on funnel metrics than Ashby or Gem.

---

## SHRM (Society for Human Resource Management)

**Publisher**: SHRM
**Focus**: Time-to-fill, cost-per-hire, offer acceptance, HR policy benchmarks
**Primary URL**: https://www.shrm.org/topics-tools/research-reports
**Notes**: Broader HR audience (not just TA), but cost-per-hire and time-to-fill
benchmarks are frequently referenced. Search site for "talent acquisition" or
"recruiting metrics".

---

## iCIMS

**Publisher**: iCIMS (ATS platform)
**Focus**: Hiring velocity, candidate experience, job application trends
**Primary URL**: https://www.icims.com/resources/
**Alternate**: https://www.icims.com/hiring-insights/
**Notes**: iCIMS Workforce Report is published quarterly. Reflects broad ATS
data across many industries, not just tech. Good for volume and velocity metrics.

---

## Korn Ferry

**Publisher**: Korn Ferry
**Focus**: Executive/leadership talent, compensation, workforce trends
**Primary URL**: https://www.kornferry.com/insights/featured-topics/talent
**Notes**: More exec-level and compensation focused. Useful for senior hire
benchmarks and total comp market data. Less focused on funnel metrics.

---

## HireVue

**Publisher**: HireVue
**Focus**: Hiring trends, video interview adoption, AI in hiring, candidate experience
**Primary URL**: https://www.hirevue.com/resources/ebooks-reports
**Notes**: Their "Global Trends Report" covers candidate expectations and
technology adoption. Useful for understanding how candidates experience the
hiring process.

## Notes on Access

Some reports are gated behind a form submission or login. If a URL leads to a
gated page:
1. Save the landing page summary and any visible stats
2. Note in the markdown file: `access: gated — landing page only`
3. Tell the user which reports need manual download and offer to process PDFs
   they upload

Most Ashby, iCIMS, and LinkedIn reports are freely accessible. Korn Ferry and
Mercer reports may require registration.
