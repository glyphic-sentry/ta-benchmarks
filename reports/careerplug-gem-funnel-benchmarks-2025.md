## Key Findings

The funnel narrows fast and gets progressively more competitive. Screening eliminates 97% of applicants before any human contact. Sourced candidates convert at measurably higher rates than inbound at every stage. Competition is intensifying — applications per hire tripled since 2021. Interview inflation (+42% more rounds per hire) compounds time-to-hire and candidate drop-off. 42% of candidates abandon processes when interview scheduling takes too long (Cronofy, 2024).
